# Problem Statement

## Background

State Bank of India (SBI) serves millions of customers through its branch network and digital banking platforms. While digital channels such as YONO and Internet Banking have simplified many banking services, customer acquisition continues to face challenges due to complex onboarding workflows and incomplete digital journeys.

Many first-time customers begin the onboarding process but are unable to complete it because of documentation issues, uncertainty about product selection, repeated verification steps, or the absence of timely guidance. Existing chatbot solutions are primarily designed to answer frequently asked questions and do not actively assist customers in completing the onboarding process.

---

## Challenges

The current customer acquisition journey presents several operational challenges:

* Customers often abandon applications before completion.
* Banking products are not always recommended based on individual needs.
* Manual document verification increases processing time.
* Customers receive limited assistance once an application is left incomplete.
* Branch staff spend significant time handling repetitive onboarding queries.

These challenges increase operational effort while reducing digital onboarding efficiency.

---

## Why Agentic AI?

Customer onboarding is not a single interaction but a sequence of dependent tasks. It requires understanding customer intent, collecting information, verifying documents, checking eligibility, scheduling KYC, and following up when required.

Instead of relying on a single conversational model, an agent-based approach allows specialized AI agents to collaborate, each responsible for a specific stage of the onboarding process. This improves decision-making, reduces manual intervention, and provides customers with a more guided and personalized experience.

---

## Objective

Design an intelligent multi-agent platform that supports customers throughout their onboarding journey, reduces application drop-offs, improves digital adoption, and helps SBI acquire customers more efficiently across its existing digital channels.
