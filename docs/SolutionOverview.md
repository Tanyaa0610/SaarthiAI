# Solution Overview

## SaarthiAI

SaarthiAI is a multi-agent customer acquisition platform designed to support SBI's digital onboarding process. Rather than functioning as a traditional chatbot, the platform coordinates multiple AI agents that work together to assist customers from their first interaction until successful onboarding.

The solution is designed to integrate with existing SBI digital channels such as YONO, the SBI website, WhatsApp Banking, and branch-assisted digital kiosks.

---

## Key Capabilities

### Intent Understanding

Identifies why a customer has approached the bank and determines the appropriate onboarding workflow.

### Customer Profiling

Builds a customer profile using demographic information, financial goals, and basic eligibility details.

### Product Recommendation

Suggests relevant SBI products based on customer requirements rather than generic recommendations.

### Eligibility Verification

Evaluates customer eligibility using predefined business rules before proceeding with onboarding.

### Intelligent Document Processing

Extracts and validates information from uploaded documents using OCR before forwarding them for verification.

### Fraud & Risk Screening

Performs basic validation checks to identify inconsistencies in submitted information.

### Guided KYC

Supports customers through the KYC process and provides clear instructions for pending actions.

### Follow-up Automation

Tracks incomplete applications and sends timely reminders to encourage successful completion.

## Current Development Stage

This repository represents the concept design and MVP planning phase prepared for the SBI Hackathon at Global FinTech Fest (GFF) 2026.

The current implementation focuses on architecture design, workflow planning, documentation, and project structure. Functional development of the multi-agent workflow is planned in subsequent stages.
